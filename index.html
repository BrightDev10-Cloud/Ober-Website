The cost of a round-the-world trip varies greatly depending on your travel style, destinations, and duration. On average, budget travelers can expect to spend $20,000-$30,000 for a year-long journey, while mid-range travelers might spend $30,000-$50,000. This typically includes flights, accommodation, food, activities, and insurance. We offer customizable packages to fit different budgets and can help you plan the perfect itinerary within your price range.

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience: all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. You can pay in full or choose our flexible payment plan options for bookings over $2,000.
